Uber Car Requirements 2020

No matter what kind of car you want to drive you will have to meet requirements to operate in a category. So, let’s talk about the most basic option.

UberX

The least expensive Uber service. Seats 4 riders. Drivers use everyday cars that are 2000 or newer.

This is a base model 4 door vehicle of a set age by the Uber corporate office. No need to look for contact customer service number. The requirements to drive UberX are basic. The following requisites are mandatory regardless of your Uber service selection.

  • So what does it take to get on the road for Uber?
  • You have to be at least 21 years old
  • You have to present an in-state (valid) drivers license
  • You have to pass a background check
  • You have to have a clean driving record
  • You must have at least 3 years of driving experience

Rideshare Car Requirements

  • A base model 4-door sedan that will seat 4 passengers, not including the Driver
  • Car insurance in-state
  • Car plates registered in-state
  • Current vehicle registration
  • No salvaged cars or marked taxi vehicles
  • Vehicle inspection must be passed
  • 2001* year model or newer
    • Los Angeles – 15-year max vehicle age (ex: 2001 can be used through 2016)
    • Atlanta – 2005
    • New York City – 2011
    • Chicago – 2001
    • Philadelphia – 2001
    • Many other cities it’s 2005 or newer

UberAssist

At Uber, we strive to create transportation options that fit everyone’s needs. Today we are announcing a new option that will allow riders needing an extra hand to request safe and reliable rides at the tap of a button, Uber Assist.

Uber Assist is designed to provide additional assistance to seniors and people with disabilities. Driver-partners are specifically trained by a third party to assist riders into vehicles and can accommodate folding wheelchairs, walkers, and scooters.

UberPool

UberPool matches with riders heading in the same direction, so they can share the ride and cost.

  • Affordable door-to-door rides
  • Carpool with others in comfortable sedans
  • Maximum 2 seats per request
